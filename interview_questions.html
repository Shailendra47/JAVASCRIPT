Interview Questions

  • Q.1 How do I get JavaScript?

    • A. You don't have to get or download JavaScript.

  • Q.2 Where can I download JavaScript?

    • A. JavaScript is already running in your browser on your computer, on your tablet, and on your smart-phone.

  • Q.3 Is JavaScript Free?

    • A. JavaScript is free to use for everyone.
